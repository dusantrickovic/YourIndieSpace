Welcome to my website!

Hello, visitor, and welcome to the safe space for aesthetics lovers! Discovering the magic and the boundless potential of the power of the internet for me has truly always been the awakening point. It was the moment when my digital self was born and ready to browse the world. But, as I was browsing, I had quickly discovered that none of the websites truly captured my heart completely. Rare were the ones who were close to acheiving that, but still, it needed a little something else. And so, I have created this website to my own liking, fully designed to be the home for all digital wanderlusters who happen to come across it. The website has a variety of forest images, beautiful mountain landscapes, excerpts of poetry and some indie song recommendations and links to additionally enhance the user experience.

I really hope you enjoy the experience and decide to stay in Your Indie Space for a little while :)

Dusan Trickovic 99/19
